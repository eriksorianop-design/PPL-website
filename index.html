PPL Split

You separate exercises into 3 movement categories. This prevents overlapping fatigue and lets muscles recover while other muscle groups train.

Push

  • Incline chest press
  • Overhead triceps extension
  • Seated shoulder press
  • Cable fly crossovers
  • Single arm triceps pushdown
  • Single arm lateral raise

Pull

  • Pull up
  • Preacher curl
  • Rear delt reverse fly
  • Bayesian curls
  • Lat pulldown
  • Seated cable row
  • Shrugs

Legs

  • Squat
  • Lying leg curl
  • Leg extension
  • Hip thurst
  • Calf press
  • Romanian deadlift
